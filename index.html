Jeremy Zhengqi Huang

I am a Human-Computer Interaction researcher and a second-year Ph.D. student at the University of Michigan Computer Science and Engineering, advised by Prof. Dhruv Jain. My research focuses on developing human-AI systems that support people with disabilities and other access needs in personalizing media and sensory environments, improving access to information, communication, and everyday experiences.

I am part of the Soundability Lab at the University of Michigan CSE. Prior to my Ph.D. studies, I graduated with a Master's in Human-Computer Interaction at the University of Michigan School of Information and a Bachelor's in Data Science and Psychology at The Ohio State University.
